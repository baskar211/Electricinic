Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?
We proudly serve the greater metropolitan area and surrounding suburbs. If you are unsure whether your location falls within our service area, please give us a call or send us an email.
Do you handle small residential jobs?
Yes, no job is too small! We handle everything from replacing a single outlet or light switch to full home rewiring and panel upgrades.
How soon can you arrive?
For emergency electrical issues, we aim to arrive within 2 hours. For non-urgent, scheduled services, we work closely with you to find the most convenient time.
Do you offer free estimates?
Yes, we provide free, no-obligation estimates for most installation and upgrade projects to ensure you know the costs upfront.
Are your technicians licensed and insured?
Yes, all our electricians are fully licensed, bonded, and insured, providing you with full protection and peace of mind during our service.
